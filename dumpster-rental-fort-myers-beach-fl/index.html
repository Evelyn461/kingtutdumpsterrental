Frequently Asked Questions

What size of dumpster will be suitable for my restaurant?

For commercial businesses that generate waste regularly, we offer various dumpster sizes to fit your needs. Our front-load or rear-load dumpsters are often the best fit for restaurants. The ideal size depends on how much waste your restaurant produces, and our team is always ready to help you assess that and recommend the right option.

Can I dispose of heavy materials into your dumpster?

Yes, you can do so. They are built durably with reinforced metal. But do note the weight limit of the dumpster you rent.

How can I make payment for a dumpster rental service?

We’ve made payment simple and convenient. You can pay directly through our website or via our mobile application.

Do you rent out your roll-off dumpsters on contract for industries?

Yes, we do. Our roll-off dumpsters are available on contract for industrial use, starting from a minimum of 3 months or more.

I have a lot of scrap metal. Do you pay for it or is there still a hauling fee?

While we do not purchase scrap metal directly, we can provide a dedicated roll off container for it. Depending on the volume and current market rates for metal, we can often offer a significantly reduced hauling fee or, in some cases, a no-cost haul for clean-loaded, high-value scrap. Please call us to discuss your specific material.

How long can I rent roll-off dumpsters?

Our roll-off dumpsters are available for short-term use and are rented out on a 7–10 day open ticket, with charges based on daily usage. If you finish early, you can schedule a pickup right away. If you’d like to keep it beyond the 7–10 days, just let our team know and we’ll work out a pricing plan that suits you.

Fort Myers Beach, FL Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

Fort Myers Beach is a town located on the North end of Estero Island in Lee County, Florida, United States. The town is on the Gulf of Mexico and is accessed from the mainland by a bridge over Estero Bay. The population was 5,582 at the 2020 census. It is part of the Cape Coral-Fort Myers, Florida Metropolitan Statistical Area. It was officially incorporated on December 31, 1995.
